Robotic Process Automation

By: Kathryn Carbonell

What is it?

RPA,

Robotic procces automation, or RPA, is a general tool that relies on AI or software robots that can be specialized to complete different tasks. It is used a lot in warehouses, customer service, accounting, financial service, healthcare, and supply chain management. Many experts say that RPA eliminates human error and speeds up production and menial assignments. RPA is predicted to revolutionize the production industry and help with greater efficiency in the production lines.

Into The Job Industry

What Jobs are there in RPA?,

Many people are worried that with the introduction of automating procceses, that it will replace jobs. While some jobs are replaced, others are created too. For example, a RPA developer is a job that was created to help make these procceses. The developer is responsible for making automation possible for the robot or program. They are able to take originally human done processes and create workflow diagrams to document correct procedures the RPA follows. The developer then needs to test and work out any bugs that may arise in the program. While robots can do many things they can not create the nessesary coding for the program. They can only do what's in their program because they can not think for themselves.
