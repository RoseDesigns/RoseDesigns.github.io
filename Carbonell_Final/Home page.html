ABOUT RPA

Key features and benifits

Computers

RPA relies heavinly on computers to instruct the robot. The computer is what runs the program that makes automation possible.

Programming

Programming is how the robot is able to do a series of tasks without fail. The program dictates how the robot moves and how it completes a task.

Jobs

With the convincence of RPA the worry of what this means for the jobs market is a big concern. While it is true the machines can replace some people, there are still limits to robotic capability. RPA also creates jobs in the making and maintence in RPA robots.

Artificial Intelligence

AI is often combined with RPA to create high funtioning robots with more capabilites than standard RPA machines. The combination of AI and RPA is where future sights are set.

RPA Developers

Leading the Way for the Future

Automation Anywhere, Inc.

Founded by Ankur Kothari, provides AI-powered software bots and has recently partnered with Saudi German Hospital (SGH) to deliver these bots for in-patient care because of the coronavirus pandemic.

Blue Prism PLC

One of the major leaders in the RPA industry that specializes in complementary analytics, automation, and decision management applications.

UiPath SRL

A Romanian RPA vendor, ranked 3rd on the Forbes Cloud 100 in 2019. The company has a large network, and has introduced a RPA developer nanodegree.

NICE

A global software solutions company, assisting organizations to enhance customer experience and business results. Within the last year it added new capabilities to its existing RPA platform to assist organizations in fast-tracking attended automation discovery, development, and human to robot collaborations.
